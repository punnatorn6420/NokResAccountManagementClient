Welcome to NokRes Account Management

NokRes Account Management is an internal administrative application designed to help authorized users efficiently manage Agent profiles, RES accounts, and related access information within the Nok Air ecosystem.

Agent Management

Create, view, and maintain Agent profiles in a structured and consistent manner.

RES Account Control

Manage RES accounts, assign primary accounts, and perform secure password resets when required.

Audit & Traceability

Review administrative actions and password rotation history for transparency and operational control.
